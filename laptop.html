Laptop prices increased

Jan 28, 2026

In recent months laptop prices have risen due to a combination of supply chain disruptions, increased demand for remote work devices, and higher component costs (notably memory and displays). Trade tariffs and shipping delays have further pressured manufacturers to raise retail prices.

This article explores the main factors, how they affect consumer choice, and tips for finding good deals or alternative upgrade paths.

Quick tips

  • Consider buying last-season models for large savings.
  • Buy during major sales (student discounts, festival sales).
  • Upgrade RAM/storage separately when possible.
